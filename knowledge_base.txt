# Knowledge Base - Voice AI Assistant

## Voice AI Assistant Hackathon 2026
The Voice AI Assistant Hackathon 2026 is an event dedicated to building the most intuitive and responsive voice-first applications.
Participants are encouraged to explore advanced combinations of ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition), RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), and TTS (Text-to-Speech).

## Technical Specifications
- **ASR**: OpenAI Whisper is used for high-accuracy speech-to-text.
- **RAG**: FAISS (Facebook AI Similarity Search) is the vector database of choice, with Sentence Transformers for embedding generation.
- **LLM**: Grok (by xAI) is the core reasoning engine, providing context-aware answers.
- **TTS**: Coqui TTS provides human-like speech synthesis for auditory responses.

## Future of Conversational AI
The future involves low-latency, emotionally aware agents.
By combining multi-modal inputs with robust retrieval systems, we can create assistants that truly understand natural human intent.

## How to use this system
1. Click the "Record Voice" button.
2. Speak clearly into your microphone for 3-5 seconds.
3. Wait for the system to process: Whisper transcribes text, RAG retrieves relevant document context, Grok generates a reply, and Coqui TTS synthesizes audio.
4. Listen to the AI's response through the Streamlit interface.
