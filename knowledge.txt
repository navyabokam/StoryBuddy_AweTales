Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.

Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that allows systems to learn from data.

Deep learning is a type of machine learning using neural networks.

Voice assistants use speech recognition and natural language processing.

The Voice AI Assistant Hackathon 2026 is an event dedicated to building intuitive and responsive voice-first applications.

Grok is the Large Language Model developed by xAI, used for high-level reasoning and conversation.

Whisper is an automatic speech recognition (ASR) system developed by OpenAI.
